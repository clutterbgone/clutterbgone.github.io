FAQ

Here is a collection of most commonly asked questions! If you still have questions please contact us and we can help get them answered.

Currently we are doing business in Weber & Davis County, but we do offer our services to other areas with a small out-of-area charge.

For your convenience we accept cash, Venmo and major credit cards.

We prohibit the loading of hazardous materials, paint, electronics, boulders, varnish, pesticides, lawn care products, aerosols, paint thinner, antifreeze, motor oil, diesel, gasoline, cleaning items, automotive products, rechargeable and lead acid batteries or any other items that the Davis Landfill does not accept.

If you are disposing of any tires, refrigerators or propane tanks please let us know, so we can plan accordingly prior to going to the dump.

We try to make your life as easy and cost effective as possible. Our You Fill, We Haul service starts at $100 + dump fees for a 24-hour rental. During that time if you require the trailer to be emptied you can coordinate with us to do extra dump runs for a $25 charge + dump fee. We just ask that you contact us with reasonable heads up time.
